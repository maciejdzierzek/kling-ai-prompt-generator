# Image-to-Video Examples

Ready-to-use I2V prompts. Each example includes notes on what motion to expect.

---

## Product Animation (Static Camera)

**Use case:** Cosmetics, perfumes, electronics - any product where subtle motion adds premium feel without distorting the subject.

```
Subtle glow pulsation on perfume bottle, gentle ambient light shifting across glass surfaces. Faint reflection movement along curved edges. Camera remains completely static. Background stable and unchanged. Minimal motion, premium feel. Duration: 5s.
```

**Expected result:** Soft light play on the bottle, no deformation of shape or text. Camera does not move. Good for luxury product ads.

---

## Portrait Animation

**Use case:** Photos of people where you want natural, lifelike movement without generating a new face.

```
Subject's hair moves gently as if touched by soft breeze. Subtle eye movement, natural micro-expressions. Clothing fabric shifts slightly. Camera static, background completely fixed. Cinematic lighting unchanged. Duration: 5s.
```

**Expected result:** Hair and fabric have soft organic motion. Eyes blink or shift subtly. Face remains recognizable from the source image.

---

## Nature Scene Loop

**Use case:** Landscape photos, background visuals for presentations, social content.

```
Leaves sway gently in breeze, water surface ripples softly, clouds drift slowly in background. Birds fly across distant sky. Camera remains static. Warm natural lighting. Peaceful, continuous motion. Duration: 10s. Aspect ratio: 16:9.
```

**Expected result:** Multiple elements animate simultaneously. Motion is organic and loops reasonably well. Great for ambient video backgrounds.

---

## Logo / Brand Animation (Minimal)

**Use case:** Static logos or branded graphics where you want elegant motion without changing the design.

```
Subtle light reflection passes across company logo, gentle ambient glow pulsation around edges. Logo and all text remain completely fixed and unchanged in position and shape. Background static. Elegant, minimal motion. Duration: 5s. Aspect ratio: 1:1.
```

**Expected result:** The logo stays readable and undistorted. Only light effects animate. Safe for brand use.

> **Tip:** Always add "all text remains fixed and unchanged" when your source image contains text you want to preserve.
